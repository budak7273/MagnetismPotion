Adds a new potion that helps with picking up items while mining or from mob farms. All players require the mod in multiplayer.

New Content

Drink the potion to increase your Item Pickup Radius. Stacks with other modifiers such as the Magnetic Ring and Trench Coat.

Known Bugs

For bug reports, please ping robb4 in the #modding-chat on the Core Keeper discord.

  • There is no "buff icon" for the potion that appears below your Health and Food bars. I assume this is because the Other/ItemPickUpRadius effect is only expected to be on equipment items.
  • The crafting recipes sometimes appear multiple times. I don't know why this is, but it doesn't seem to break anything. Might be a bug with CoreLib.
